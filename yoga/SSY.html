Sukha Pranayama (Nadi Shodhana) in SSY

SSY's Sukha (easy/comfortable) Pranayama is a gentle alternate nostril breathing (no fixed ratio/kumbhaka; ~3 min before Mahat), balancing Ida/Pingala nadis into Sushumna for calm prana flow. Vajrasana (knees apart/toes together/spine erect), eyes closed, right hand Vishnu/Mrigi Mudra (thumb right nostril, ring+pinky left; index+middle rest forehead). Gentle pressure; exhale fully first.

Posture & Setup Verification

Vajrasana: Mirror—spine straight (wall touch test), shoulders relaxed (ear-shoulder drop), left hand Jnana Mudra on knee (thumb+index circle).

Hand Mudra: Thumb seals right, ring+pinky left (no pinch—soft close); elbow supported if long, wrist straight (no bend).

General Check: Nose clear (neti if blocked); room quiet, empty stomach. Self-video vs. SSY Pranayama Shibir demos (e.g., timestamps show sequence).

Detailed Steps

  1. Prep: Exhale both nostrils; close right (thumb).
  2. Inhale left (gentle/deep to abdomen).
  3. Close left (ring+pinky), release right.
  4. Exhale right fully.
  5. Inhale right.
  6. Close right, release left; exhale left. (1 cycle; repeat 3 min / 20-30 cycles).

End: Hands knees, normal breath awareness.

Breath Verification

Flow: Smooth/equal volume (left=right duration; ~4-6s each); audible soft "ha" exhale optional (no force).

Test: Mirror nostrils equal open; hand nostril feels air (no leak). Flow balanced (no dominant side—practice evens).

Feel: Cooling channels, mind steady (no dizziness = correct). Metronome ~10 breaths/min; wrong if strain/headache—soften.

Cycle Accuracy Checks

Step Mirror/Feel Test Common Error Fix
Close Right / In Left Air only left (hand test); belly fills first Thumb loose—firmer seal
Switch / Ex Right Right opens fully; equal exhale length Anticipate—exhale complete
In Right / Ex Left Symmetry; no shoulder rise Uneven flow—slow left nostril
Full Cycle 20 cycles ~3 min; calm post Rushed—count breaths

Overall Tips: Record/compare SSY videos (e.g., "Siddh Samadhi Yog Pranayama Shibir"); correct = Ida/Pingala harmony (steady mind/prana). For asthma, enhances lung balance safely. Teacher feedback via camp ideal.

Sectional Breathing (Vibhagya Pranayamas) in SSY

SSY's sectional breathing sequentially activates lower (Kanishta), middle (Madhyama), and upper (Jyeshta) lung lobes using hand pressure and 4:2:5:2 ratio (inhale 4s : hold-in 2s : exhale 5s : hold-out 2s). Practice morning, empty stomach, eyes closed, Vajrasana posture, slow/full/no jerks/throat sound on exhale. 7 rounds each; video demos confirm accuracy (e.g., YouTube: "Sectional Breathing - Vibhagiya Pranayama").

Common Posture Verification (All)

Vajrasana: Knees apart (hip-width), big toes touching, heels up (sit on heels if comfy), spine straight (no slouch—check side mirror/wall), hands on thighs (palms up), chin parallel to floor.

Breath Check: Nose only, audible throat "ha" exhale (like fogging mirror), no mouth/shoulder movement, abdomen/chest moves independently per section. Record self/video; match SSY instructor demos (slow, no strain).

Ratio Test: Use metronome app (60bpm: inhale 4 beats, etc.); belly/chest rises visibly without neck tension. Dizzy? Stop/shorten.

Kanishta (Lower Lobe - Abdomen)

Focus: Diaphragm/abdomen only (no chest rise).

  • Palms flat/horizontal, thumbs+index fingers line lowest ribs sides, elbows forward (light pressure).
  • Exhale: Hands compress inward (navel to spine).
  • Inhale (4s): Stomach bulges, pushes hands out (hand on chest stays still).
  • Hold-in (2s), exhale (5s, throat sound), hold-out (2s).

Verify Accuracy:

  • Mirror: Abdomen expands/contracts solo; chest flat.
  • Hand Test: One hand navel (rises), one chest (immobile).
  • Feel: Diaphragm drop like balloon; no rib flare. Wrong if shoulders lift.

Madhyama (Middle Lobe - Chest)

Focus: Ribcage/chest sides (minimal abdomen).

  • Thumbs armpits (behind pecs), palms high chest (sternum level), elbows shoulder-height.
  • Exhale: Hands/palms together (front pressure).
  • Inhale (4s): Chest expands sideways, hands part.
  • Hold-in/exhale/hold-out as above.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Mirror: Ribs widen (lateral), no belly/shoulder rise.
  • Touch: Hands sense expansion; abdomen passive.
  • Sound: Deeper "ha"; wrong if neck strains or belly moves first.

Jyeshta (Upper Lobe - Shoulders/Clavicular)

Focus: Collarbones/upper chest (subtle).

  • Raise chest high, chin to chest (Jalandhara light), arms overhead/back (hands light/uncrossed, palms up-ish).
  • Press head down gently, re-raise chest (shoulder blades pinch).
  • Inhale (4s): Upper lungs fill (shoulder/heart expansion).
  • Hold-in/exhale (throat sound)/hold-out.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Mirror: Collarbones lift minimally; no big chest/abdomen wave.
  • Feel: Tingling shoulders; arms relaxed (no tension).
  • Check: Hand on clavicles rises; lower body still. Avoid hyper-extension.

Dhouti (Cooling after Jyeshta, 3x)

Cleanses post-upper breathing.

  • Blow out forcefully 5x (hold empty lungs).
  • Bend forward, crown to floor (chin tucked tight).
  • Hold shoonyaka (empty retention, relaxed).
  • Rise halfway (exhale done), inhale normally.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Feel: Cooling throat/chest vibration; no strain (pain? Stop).
  • Mirror: Back straight bend, no knee lift.
  • Posture: Vajrasana intact; breath auto-resumes smooth.

Mudra Pranayamas in SSY

SSY mudra pranayamas use specific hand gestures (tight mudra only; arms/shoulders/neck loose) on mid-thighs (elbows by body) to direct prana to body regions, with 4:2:5:2 ratio (inhale 4s : hold-in 2s : exhale 5s throat "ha" : hold-out 2s). Vajrasana, eyes closed, chest erect/back straight. 2-3 min each (7 homework rounds); notice max expansion per lobe. Video verify: Search "SSY Mudra Pranayama" (e.g., Telugu demo shows sequence).

Common Verification (All Mudras)

Posture: Mirror—spine straight (no lean), elbows touch ribs, shoulders dropped (ear-shoulder-roll check), hands mid-thigh (not knees).

Mudra: Tight grip (feel pulse/energy), fingers aligned (no gaps), palms as specified (down/up).

Breath: Metronome for ratio; throat fogs mirror on exhale; expansion matches lobe (hand-on-body test). Self-video vs. SSY instructors; correct = prana tingle, no fatigue.

Sequence: Chin → Chinmaya → Adi → Meru-Danda → Poorna; transition eyes closed, no stop.

Chin Mudra (Lower - Abdomen)

Formation: Thumb + index tips touch (circle), other 3 fingers straight/extended.

  • Palms down mid-thighs.
  • Inhale: Abdomen expands (max here).
  • 4:2:5:2; exhale compresses navel.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Mirror/Feel: Belly sole mover (chest flat); hand navel rises, chest hand still.
  • Wrong: Chest/shoulder joins—relax more. Energy feels "grounded lower body".

Chinmaya Mudra (Middle - Chest)

Formation: Thumb + index circle, other 3 fingers fold to palm base (not full fist).

  • Palms down mid-thighs.
  • Inhale: Chest sides/ribs expand (max here).
  • Full 4:2:5:2.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Mirror: Lateral rib flare, abdomen passive.
  • Test: Chest hand parts, belly immobile; "ha" deeper.
  • Wrong: Belly dominates—focus middle prana.

Adi Mudra (Upper - Shoulders)

Formation: Thumb center palm, 4 fingers close over (light fist), palm down.

  • Mid-thighs.
  • Inhale: Shoulders/upper chest (clavicle pit-throat up).
  • 4:2:5:2.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Feel: Shoulder tingle/expansion; mirror collarbones subtle lift.
  • Check: No lower movement; upper prana "light".
  • Wrong: Fist loose/tight neck—reform/relax.

Meru-Danda Mudra (Spine/Back Ribs)

Formation: 4 fingers tips to palm base (folded), thumb straight up/perpendicular.

  • Hands mid-thighs, thumbs up.
  • Inhale: Back/spine ribs expand.
  • 4:2:5:2.

Verify Accuracy:

  • Feel: Posterior expansion (wall-touch back test).
  • Mirror: Thumbs vertical, no lean; spine "lengthens".
  • Wrong: Thumbs tilt—straighten; front dominates.

Poorna Mudra (Full Body)

Formation: Fists together (knuckle/ring-finger level), palms up on abdomen; straighten arms, elbows lock, shoulders up (chest raised).

  • Fists press navel on exhale.
  • Inhale: Whole torso expands.
  • 4:2:5:2 (arms straight throughout).

Verify Accuracy:

  • Mirror: Arms extended (no bend), shoulders elevated evenly, fists adjacent.
  • Feel: Uniform body fill; exhale presses abdomen.
  • Test: Video full demo; integrated expansion all lobes.
  • Wrong: Elbows drop/abdomen only—lock/raise.

Tips: Daily homework builds accuracy; prana flow confirms (warmth/joy). Attend SSY camp/teacher for hands-on check; safe for asthma adjunct.
