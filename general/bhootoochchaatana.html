ಭೂತೋಚ್ಛಾಟನ (bhootOchchaaTana)

Smell akshata using deer mudra, then throw it below your left arm. If your partner is on your left, throw it below their left arm.

ಉತ್ತಿಷ್ಠಂತು ಭೂತಪಿಶಾಚಾಃ ಏತೇ ಭೂಮಿ ಭಾರಕಾಃ
uttishThamtu bhūta-piśācāḥ ete bhūmi-bhārakāḥ
 ಏತೇಷಾಮವಿರೋಧೇನ ಬ್ರಹ್ಮಕರ್ಮ ಸಮಾರಭೇ
eteṣhām-avirodhena brahma-karma samārabhe
