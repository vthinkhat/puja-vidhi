This page provides an overview of the Puja Rules section of this project. Here you'll find guidelines, best practices, and helpful notes for performing or documenting puja (ritual worship) procedures.

1/3 Before sitting for puja

  1. External & internal cleanliness
  2. Take bath, wear washed clothes
  3. Keep puja mandir clean.
  4. Keep your mind clean and free from distractions.
  5. Same time, same place, every day (there is a big reason).
Note: External & internal cleanliness

2/3 While doing the puja

  1. Is our gaze/sight (drishti) standing on the deity; even one japa will be effective.
  2. This is more important than the previous step (step 1/3)
Note: If you do it right, you will try to find out the meaning of the mantra. You puja has gone up on step.

3/3 After the puja

  1. Puja is like charing your mobile; you need to keep it charged.
  2. Sathyam, Dharmam and Dhayaa must be followed.
Note: Nothing else is needed.
