Permanent Homam Materials

  • Havan Kund (metal or brick)
  • Havan Spoon (wood or metal)
  • Palagai (wooden plank)
  • Full metal knife (secateur will do)
  • Paving tile (add IKEA soft legs pads to avoid scratches)
  • Sand (between paving tile and havan kund)

Consumable Homam Materials

  • Firewood - 50 small sticks
  • Dry coconut pieces - 2 (break small one to 4 pieces)
  • Cardamom / Elaichi - 2
  • Pacha Karpooram / Natural Camphor - 1
  • Karpooram / Camphor - 10 pieces or 1 big block
  • Agarbhathi / Incense - 1 pack
  • Honey - 3 tsp
  • Sandal powder - 1 tsp (good quality, not Chandan tablet)
  • Cowdung cake (dried) - 2 slabs
  • Ashwatha (Peepal Tree) sticks - 6 + 4
  • Cow Ghee - 200 ml
  • Gingelly Oil - 20 ml
  • Turmeric stick - 1

Difficult to Find Consumables

  • Palash sticks or seeds - 1
    Butea monosperma (Palash, Flame of the forest, Dhak)
    - Kannada: ಮುತ್ತುಗದ ಮರ (Muttuga mara)
    - Telugu: మోదుగ చెట్టు (Moduga Chettu)
    - Tamil: புரசு (Purasu), பலாசு (Palasu), முருக்கன் மரம் (Murukkan maram)
    - Sanskrit: पलाश (Palāśa), किंशुक (Kiṃśuka)
  • Saraswati/Bramhi leaves - 4
    Centella Asiatica (Pennywort, Brahmi, Saraswati Soppu)
    - Kannada: ಬ್ರಾಹ್ಮಿ ಸೊಪ್ಪು (Brahmi Soppu), ಸರಸ್ವತಿ ಸೊಪ್ಪು (Saraswati Soppu), ಇಲಿಕಿವಿ ಸೊಪ್ಪು (Ilikivi Soppu)
    - Telugu: సరస్వతీ ఆకు (Sarasvati Aku), మండూకపర్ణి (Mandukaparni)
    - Tamil: வல்லாரை கீரை (Vallarai Keerai)
    - Sanskrit: मण्डूकपर्णी (Mandukaparni), ब्राह्मी (Brahmi)
  • Dried Lotus flower - 1
  • Giloy Stem / Tippa Teega / Amruthavalli - 1
    Tinospora cordifolia (Giloy, Guduchi)
    - Kannada: ಅಮೃತಬಳ್ಳಿ (Amruthaballi), ಉಗಣಿಬಳ್ಳಿ (Uganiballi)
    - Telugu: తిప్పతీగ (Tippa Teega)
    - Tamil: சீந்தில் கொடி (Seenthil Kodi), அம்ருതவল்லி (Amruthavalli)
    - Sanskrit: गिलोय (Giloy), गुडुची (Guduchi), अमृता (Amrita)
  • Uttareni (Apaamarga) - 1
    Achyranthes aspera (Apamarga, Chaff-flower)
    - Kannada: ಉತ್ತರಾಣಿ (Uttaraani), ಗೊರವಬಳ್ಳಿ (Gorwiballi), ಕರಿಹಂಬು (Karihambu)
    - Telugu: ఉత್తరేణి (Uttareni), అపామార్గము (Apamargamu)
    - Tamil: நாயுருவி (Nayuruvi)
    - Sanskrit: अपामार्ग (Apamarga)
  • Jammi (Shami tree) - 1
    Prosopis cineraria (Khejri, Shami, King of Desert)
    - Kannada: ಬನ್ನಿ ಮರ (Banni mara), ಪೆರುಂಬೆ (Perumbe), ಶಮೀ ಪತ್ರೆ (Shamee patre), ವುನ್ನೆ (Vunne)
    - Telugu: జమ్మి చెట్టు (Jammi Chettu)
    - Tamil: வன்னி (Vanni), சீமை கருவேலை (Seemai Karuvelai)
    - Sanskrit: शमीवृक्ष (Shamivruksha)
  • White Jilleedu (get White Arka sticks) - 1
    Calotropis procera (Crown Flower, Milkweed)
    - Kannada: ಎಕ್ಕದ ಗಿಡ (Ekkad Gida), ಎಕ್ಕಿ ಗಿಡ (Ekki Gida), अर्क (Arka)
    - Telugu: జిల్లెడు (Jilledu), తెల్లజిల్లేడు (Tellajilledu)
    - Tamil: வெள்ளெருக்கு (Vellarukku)
    - Sanskrit: अर्क (Arka), रक्तार्कः (Rakta Arka), मदर (Madar), आक (Aak)
