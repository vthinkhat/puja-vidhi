ಪಲ್ಲವಿ (Refrain)

O Vishnu, who rides Garuḍa! May your divine lotus feet always shine in my heart and consciousness. Please remove all my sufferings and destroy the sins that weigh down my soul. I surrender to you completely — body, mind, and heart.
  • ಗರುಡಗಮನ: You who ride Garuda
  • ತವ ಚರಣಕಮಲಮ್: Your lotus feet
  • ಇಹ: Here (in this life)
  • ಮನಸಿ: In my mind
  • ಲಸತು: May it shine / dwell
  • ಮಮ ನಿತ್ಯಂ: In me always
  • ಮಮ ತಾಪಮ್ ಅಪಾಕುರು: Remove my sufferings, O Lord
  • ಮಮ ಪಾಪಮ್ ಅಪಾಕುರು: Remove my sins, O Lord

ಅಂತರಾ ೧ (Antarā 1)

O Lord with lotus-like eyes, worshipped by Brahma, praised by Indra, Shiva, and all divine beings — may your sacred feet uplift me. You are the refuge of the gods; please relieve my pain and purify my heart of all sins.
  • ಜಲಜ-ನಯನ: Lotus-eyed one
  • ವಿಧಿನಮ್: Brahma (the Creator)
  • ಉಚಿಹರಹರಮುಖ: Praised by Indra, Shiva, and others
  • ವಿಬುಧ-ವಿನುತ: Worshipped by celestial beings
  • ಪದಪದ್ಮ: Your lotus feet

ಅಂತರಾ ೨ (Antarā 2)

You who rest on the serpent bed of eternity, source of all creation — the very father of love itself — I bow to you. Remove the deep fear I carry of birth and death. Lead me to peace beyond the cycle of suffering.
  • ಭುಜಗ-ಶಯನ: One who reclines on the serpent bed (Ananta/Shesha)
  • ಭವ: Worldly existence (samsara)
  • ಮದನ-ಜನಕ: Father of Cupid (as Vishnu/Narayana)
  • ಮಮ ಜನನ-ಮರಣ: My cycle of birth and death
  • ಭಯ-ಹಾರಿ: Remover of fear

ಅಂತರಾ ೩ (Antarā 3)

Wielder of the divine conch and discus, destroyer of dark forces and demons — you are the protector of all worlds. Be my refuge too. Cleanse me of my past and ease the burdens that life has placed on me.
  • ಶಂಖ-ಚಕ್ರ-ಧರ: Holder of the conch and discus
  • ದುಷ್ಟ-ದೈತ್ಯ-ಹರ: Destroyer of wicked demons
  • ಸರ್ವ-ಲೋಕ-ಶರಣ: Refuge for all the worlds

ಅಂತರಾ ೪ (Antarā 4)

Lord of countless divine virtues, savior of those with no support — you shattered the forces that threatened the devas. Likewise, destroy the inner enemies of ego, desire, and ignorance in me. Bring me to your divine shelter.
  • ಅಗಣಿತ-ಗುಣ-ಗಣ: Possessor of countless virtues
  • ಅಶರಣ-ಶರಣದ: Giver of shelter to the helpless
  • ವಿದಲಿತ ಸುರ-ರಿಪು-ಜಾಲ: Crusher of enemies of the devas

ಅಂತರಾ ೫ (Antarā 5)

O Lord of boundless compassion, protect your dearest devotee — Śrī Bhāratī Tīrtha — and all who seek you with faith. Grant us your grace, remove our suffering, and make us worthy of your eternal presence.
  • ಭಕ್ತ-ವರ್ಯಮ್: Great among devotees
  • ಇಹ: In this world
  • ಭೂರಿ-ಕರುಣಯಾ: With immense compassion
  • ಪಾಹಿ: Protect
  • ಭಾರತೀ-ತೀರ್ಥಮ್: (The devotee) Sri Bharati Tirtha
