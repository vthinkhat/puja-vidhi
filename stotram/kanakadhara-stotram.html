ಗಣಪತಿ ಪ್ರಾರ್ಥನ (Ganapati Prarthana)

वन्दे वन्दारु मन्दारमिन्दिरानन्द कन्दलं अमन्दानन्द सन्दोह बन्धुरं सिन्धुराननम् ವಂದೇ ವಂದಾರು ಮಂದಾರಮಿಂದಿರಾನಂದ ಕಂದಲಂ ಅಮಂದಾನಂದ ಸಂದೋಹ ಬಂಧುರಂ ಸಿಂಧುರಾನನಂ vande vandāru mandāram indirā-ānanda kandhalam amanda-ānanda sandoha bandhuram sindhura-ānanam I bow to -- devotees -- celestial tree -- Joy of Indira (Lakshmi) -- source of happiness Immense bliss -- mass/collection -- beautiful -- one with vermilion face (Ganesha) I bow to Lord Ganesha, whose face shines like vermilion, who is like the heavenly Mandāra tree to His devotees, the very source of joy for Goddess Lakshmi herself, and who is adorned with an abundance of immense bliss and beauty.
