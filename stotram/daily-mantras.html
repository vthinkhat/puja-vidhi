ನಿತ್ಯ ಮಂತ್ರ (Daily Mantra)

ಪ್ರಭಾತ ಶ್ಲೋಕಃ (Prabhaata Sloka)

Today, may my actions invite Lakshmi's prosperity, Saraswati's wisdom, and align with Govinda's dharma. Before facing the world, I honour the divine power in my hands — God acts through me. Let this day be purposeful and sacred.

ಕರಾಗ್ರೇ ವಸತೇ ಲಕ್ಷ್ಮೀಃ ಕರಮಧ್ಯೇ ಸರಸ್ವತೀ |
kara agre vasate lakshmi, kara madhye saraswati |
Imagine golden light at your fingertips (Lakshmi: abundance)
A serene white glow in your palm (Saraswati: knowledge, purity)
ಕರಮೂಲೇ ತು ಗೋವಿಂದಃ ಪ್ರಭಾತೇ ಕರದರ್ಶನಮ್ ||
kara muuletu govinda, prabhaatae kara darshanam ||
A deep blue, strong radiance at your wrist (Govinda: strength, sustenance)
Then fold your hands in Namaskara with devotion and humility.

ಪ್ರಭಾತ ಭೂಮಿ ಶ್ಲೋಕಃ (Prabhaata Bhoomi Sloka)

ಸಮುದ್ರ-ವಸನೇ ದೇವೀ ಪರ್ವತ-ಸ್ತನ-ಮಂಡಲೇ |
samudra vasane devee, parvata stana mandale |
ವಿಷ್ಣುಪತ್ನಿ ನಮಸ್ತುಭ್ಯಂ ಪಾದಸ್ಪರ್ಶಂ ಕ್ಷಮಸ್ವಮೆ ||
vishnu patnee namastubhyam, paada sparsham kshama-swa-mae ||

ಸ್ನಾನ ಶ್ಲೋಕಃ (Snaana Sloka)

ಗಂಗೇ ಚ ಯಮುನೇ ಚೈವ ಗೋದಾವರಿ ಸರಸ್ವತಿ |
gangae cha yamunae chaiva, godavari saraswati |
 ನರ್ಮದೇ ಸಿಂಧು ಕಾವೇರಿ ಜಲೇಸ್ಮಿನ್ ಸನ್ನಿಧಿಂ ಕುರು ||
narmadae sindhu kaveri, jalae'smin sannidhim kuru ||
Ganga, Yamuna, Godavari, Saraswathi, Narmada, Sindhu, Kaveri, May all these have a holy confluence in this water.
