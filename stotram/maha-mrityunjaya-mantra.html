ಮಹಾಮೃತ್ಯುಂಜಯ ಮಂತ್ರಂ (Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra)


ಓಂ ತ್ರ್ಯಂಬಕಂ ಯಜಾಮಹೇ ಸುಗಂಧಿಂ ಪುಷ್ಟಿ॒ವರ್ಧನಂ |
tryambakam yajaamahee sugandhim pushti-vardhanam |
ಯಜಾಮಹೇ: I invoke
ಸುಗಂಧಿಂ: fragrant
ಪುಷ್ಟಿ॒ವರ್ಧನಂ: strength-enhancing
ತ್ರ್ಯಂಬಕಂ: three-eyed one
ಉರ್ವಾರುಕಮಿವ ಬಂಧನಾನ್ಮೃತ್ಯೋರ್ಮುಕ್ಷೀಯ ಮಾಽಮೃತಾತ್ ||
urvaarukam-iva bandhanaan-mrutyoor-muksheeya maa-mrutaat ||
ರ್ಮುಕ್ಷೀಯ: Liberate me
ಮಾಽಮೃತಾತ್: Not from immortality
ಬಂಧನಾನ್ಮೃತ್ಯೋ: from death's grip
ಉರ್ವಾರುಕಮಿವ: like a melon (that release from the plant)

Dyaana

For best results, meditate on the form associated with this mantra, a process known as dhyaanam.

I meditate upon that form of shiva, who has his wife by his side, known by the name mrutyunjaya, who has three eyes. He is purifying himself with water flowing down onto this head from two pots, each pot held by two hands. With two more hands he is holding two more pots in his lap. In his other two hands, he is holding a rudraksha mala, and holding mrugi mudra, which is representative of purification. On the top of his head, is the moon, from which peeyuusha or nectar is flowing down.

ಹಸ್ತಾಂಭೋಜ ಯುಗಸ್ಥ ಕುಂಭಯುಗಲಾದುದಧತ್ಯ ತೋಯಂ ಶಿರ:
hastAmbhOja yugastha mumbhayugaLAt-uddhrutya tOyam shirah |
(Lotus hands) (two Pairs) (with two pots) (water) (head)
हस्ताम्भोज (Lotus Hands) युगस्थ (Two Pairs) कुँभयुगलादुदधत्य (With two pots) तोयं (Water) शिर: (Head)

ಸಿಚಂತಂ ಕರಯೋರ್ಯುಗೇನ ದಧತಂ ಸ್ವಾಂಕೆ ಸಕುಂಭೌ ಕರೌ ।
sinchantam karayOryugEna dadhatam-svAnke sakumbhau karau |
(pouring) (with two hands) (holding)(In lap) (Two pots in Hands)
सिचन्तं (Pouring) करयोर्युगेन (With two hands) दधतं (Holding) स्वांके (In Lap) सकुम्भौ करौ (Two Pots in Hands)

ಅಕ್ಷಸ್ತ್ರಡ್ಮೃಗಹಸ್ತಮಂಬುಜಗತಂಮೂರ್ಧಸ್ಥ ಚಂದ್ರಸ್ತ್ರವತ್
akshasrak mruga-hastamambuja-gatam mUrdhastha chandram sravat |
(Rudraksha Mala) (Mrgu Mudra) (Lotus like) (moon on the head)
अक्षस्त्रड् (Rudraksha Mala) मृगहस्त (Mrgu Mudra) मंबुजगतं (Lotus Like) मूर्धस्थ चन्द्र (Moon on the head)

ಪೀಯೂಷೋತ್ರ ತನುಂ ಭಜೇ ಸಗಿರಿಜಂ ಮೃತ್ತ್ಯುಂಜಯಂ ತ್ರ್ಯಂಬಕಮ್
pIyUshOtra tanum bhojE sagirijam mrutyunjayam tryambakam |
(Pouring) (Nectar) (Body) (I Pray) (with Girija) (victor of Death) (Three Eyed Shiva)
स्त्रवत् (Pouring) पीयूषोत्र (Nectar) तनुं (Body) भजे (I pray) सगिरिजं (with Girija) मृत्युन्जयं (Victor of Death) त्र्यम्बकम् (Three Eyed Shiva)
