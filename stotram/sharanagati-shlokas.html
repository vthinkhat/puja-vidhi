ಶರಣಾಗತಿ ಶೋಲ್ಕ (Sharanagati Sholka)


Explanation

Read these 2 shlokas from Durga Saptashati to come out of the regret and guilt that are bothering you, because of your own past mistakes and choices, which are not letting you live your life to the fullest. When you place that burden at Mother's feet, she gives you the strength to forgive yourself, the courage to move on, and the power to begin again, all the while holding you in her grace.

Shloka 1

ಶರಣಾಗತ ದೀನಾರ್ತ ಪರಿತ್ರಾಣ ಪರಾಯಣೇ ।
ಸರ್ವಸ್ಯಾರ್ತಿಹರೇ ದೇವಿ ನಾರಾಯಣಿ ನಮೋಸ್ತು ತೇ ॥

शरणागतदीनार्तपरित्राणपरायणे ।
सर्वस्यार्तिहरॆ देवि नारायणि नमोऽस्तु ते ॥

śaraṇāgata-dīnārta-paritrāṇa-parāyaṇe ।
sarvasyārti-hare devi nārāyaṇi namo 'stu te ॥

Shloka 2

ಅಪಾರಾಧ ಸಹಸ್ರಾಣಿ ಕ್ರಿಯಂತೇ ಹರ್ಣಿಶಂ ಮಯಾ ।
ದಾಸೋಯಮಿತಿ ಮಾಂ ಮತ್ವಾ ಕ್ಷಮಸ್ವ ಪರಮೇಶ್ವರಿ ॥

अपाराधसहस्राणि क्रियन्तेऽहर्निशं मया ।
दासोऽयमिति मां मत्वा क्षमस्व परमेश्वरी ॥

aparādha-sahasrāṇi kriyante 'har-niśaṁ mayā ।
dāso 'yam iti māṁ matvā kṣamasva parameśvarī ॥
